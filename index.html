About AquaVision

At UAPB Fish Nutrigenomics & AI Lab, we believe in the transformative power of precision science and the boundless potential it unlocks for aquaculture communities across Arkansas and beyond.

Our mission is to foster research that empowers farmers, drives food security, and advances the science of aquatic species detection through deep learning and computer vision.

Advancing AI-powered aquaculture research for underserved communities
